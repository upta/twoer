## Architecture Highlights

- **File Organization:** `core/` → `entities/` → `battlefield/` → `systems/` → `ui/` → `level/`
- **State Management:** PhaseManager drives phase transitions via signals
- **Economy:** Per-level gold, per-battle mana (+15 per checkpoint)
- **Collision:** Layer-based separation with proper masks for unit/defense interaction
- **AI:** Deterministic heuristic (repair all walls, upgrade highest-damage tower)
- **Win Conditions:** Any unit reaches 1150px threshold
- **Lose Conditions:** All deployed units dead AND deployment complete
